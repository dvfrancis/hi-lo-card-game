Higher-or-lower is a deceptively simple card game where the player has to decide if the next card is higher or lower than their own card.

The player places a wager on whether or not they can guess the next card correctly until they guess all cards correctly, or they lose the round - and their wager! The game ends when all cards have been played, or the player has used up all of their points.

Higher-or-lower premium allows the player to make additional wagers to increase your winnings.

Players can opt to predict the outcome of the game and, if successful, their winnings are multiplied by a set amount.

As well as placing your usual wager, you can also earn additional points by predicting certain outcomes.

There are four predictions you can choose to make:

  • A specific suit - if you predict the next card's suit correctly, your winning points are doubled.
  • A matching card - if you successfully predict a matching card, your winning points are multiplied by five.
  • A specific card - if you successfully predict a specific card, your winning points are multiplied by 50.
  • A group of cards - if you successfully predict a sequence of cards, your winning points are multiplied by 100!

Both are determined randomly at the start of each game, and you will see the limits displayed on the game screen.
