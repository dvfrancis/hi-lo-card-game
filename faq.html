Hi-Lo is a deceptively simple card game where the player has to decide if the next card is higher or lower than their own card.

The player places a wager on whether or not they can guess the next card correctly until they guess all cards correctly, or they lose the round - and their wager! The game ends when all cards have been played, or the player has used up all of their points.

  1. In each round you will be presented with five cards - only the first will be face-up.
  2. Before you make each guess, you will be asked to place a wager using the buttons on the screen. Your wager cannot be lower than one or higher than your total points.
  3. When you have made your wager you will be asked to predict whether the next card is higher or lower than the current card.
  4. If you guess correctly, you will repeat the first three steps until you have correctly guessed all cards.
  5. When you have correctly guessed all cards, your wager is added to your total points; congratulations on winning the round!
  6. If you guess incorrectly, the current round ends and you will be asked if you want to continue to the next round, or exit the game.
  7. The game will end after completing 10 rounds - at this point the card pack will be empty - if this happens, you will be asked if you want to start a new game, or exit the game.
  8. The game will end if you use all of your points - if this happens, you will be asked if you want to start a new game, or exit the game.

Both are determined randomly at the start of each game, and you will see the limits displayed on the game screen.
